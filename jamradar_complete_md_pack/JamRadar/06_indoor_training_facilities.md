# 06 - Indoor Training Facilities

## Could This Work for Indoor Ski/Snowboard Treadmill Places?

Yes. Indoor ski and snowboard treadmill facilities are a very strong fit for JamRadar.

They may actually be one of the best ways to make the app useful year-round because they operate outside the normal winter resort season.

These facilities often need to promote:

- Beginner lessons
- Private coaching
- Freestyle training
- Race training
- Pre-season tune-ups
- Summer programs
- Youth camps
- Adult learn-to-ski sessions
- Snowboard technique sessions
- Balance and edging classes
- Off-season athlete development

## Why Indoor Facilities Fit the App

Indoor training facilities have a discovery problem similar to mountains.

Potential customers may not know:

- The facility exists
- What it offers
- When sessions are available
- Whether it supports snowboarders
- Whether beginners are welcome
- Whether it offers freestyle training
- Whether it has memberships or drop-ins
- Whether it runs off-season programs

JamRadar can help them reach users who already care about skiing and snowboarding.

## Indoor Facility Profile Page

Each indoor facility should have a profile with:

- Facility name
- Location
- Photos/videos
- Training equipment type
- Ski support
- Snowboard support
- Coaching options
- Beginner-friendly label
- Freestyle-friendly label
- Pricing
- Booking link
- Hours
- Age requirements
- Contact info
- Reviews, later phase

## Indoor Session Listings

Indoor facilities should be able to list sessions like events.

Example session types:

- Beginner ski treadmill lesson
- Beginner snowboard treadmill lesson
- Freestyle rail prep session
- Carving technique clinic
- Race training block
- Pre-season tune-up program
- Summer snowboard camp
- Private coaching slot
- Kids training session
- Adult group lesson

## Why This Helps Off-Season Retention

During summer and fall, resort events may slow down. Indoor facilities can keep the app active with:

- Training schedules
- Camps
- Pre-season programs
- Skill development clinics
- Gear tune-up events
- Dryland training
- Video analysis sessions

## Monetization With Indoor Facilities

Indoor facilities could pay for:

- Monthly listing subscription
- Featured facility placement
- Promoted training sessions
- Booking link tracking
- Lead generation
- Package promotion
- Seasonal campaigns

Potential pricing:

- Free profile
- $29/month for basic listing tools
- $99/month for promoted facility tools
- $199/month for multi-location or high-volume facilities

## Extra Indoor-Specific Features

### Skill Progress Tracking

Users could track:

- First lesson completed
- Carving progress
- Switch riding practice
- Rail prep progress
- Jump prep progress
- Instructor notes

This could make JamRadar more than an event app.

### Training Goals

Users could set goals:

- Learn to snowboard before winter
- Improve carving
- Prepare for park season
- Train for a race
- Get comfortable before a ski trip

### Facility Packages

Facilities could sell or promote:

- 5-session packs
- Beginner bundles
- Pre-season bootcamps
- Parent/kid packages
- Team training programs

## Recommended MVP Support for Indoor Facilities

Include indoor training in the MVP, but keep it simple:

- Indoor facility profile
- Indoor session event type
- Booking link
- Notifications for nearby indoor sessions
- Filter: indoor/outdoor

Do not build full booking inside the app at first. Link out to their booking system.
