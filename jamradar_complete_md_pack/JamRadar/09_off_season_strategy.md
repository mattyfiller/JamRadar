# 09 - Off-Season Strategy

## Why Off-Season Matters

A ski and snowboard event app can become seasonal if it only focuses on winter resort events.

To survive year-round, JamRadar needs adjacent off-season activity.

## Best Off-Season Categories

### 1. Indoor Ski/Snowboard Training

This is the strongest off-season bridge.

Features:

- Indoor treadmill lessons
- Pre-season training
- Freestyle prep
- Race training
- Camps
- Coaching sessions

### 2. Skateboarding

Skateboarding is a natural extension.

Events:

- Skate jams
- Best trick contests
- Shop nights
- Skatepark competitions
- Film premieres
- Product drops
- Lessons

### 3. MTB and Bike Parks

Many ski resorts operate as bike parks in summer.

Events:

- Downhill races
- Enduro events
- Skills clinics
- Demo days
- Trail build days
- Bike park festivals

### 4. BMX and Dirt Jump

Overlaps with freestyle culture.

Events:

- Dirt jump jams
- Pump track races
- BMX contests
- Skills sessions

### 5. Gear Swaps and Shop Events

Year-round utility.

Events:

- Gear swaps
- Tuning clinics
- Wax nights
- Used gear sales
- Demo days
- Clearance sales

### 6. Film Nights and Community Events

Great for building culture.

Events:

- Ski films
- Snowboard films
- Skate video premieres
- Brand events
- Athlete talks
- Season kickoff parties

## Season Watch Feature

Users can follow mountains and get notified for:

- Opening day announcements
- Park build updates
- Season pass deadlines
- Early bird pass pricing
- Job postings
- Volunteer days
- Demo days
- First rail setup
- Closing day events

## Off-Season App Positioning

Winter:

> Find rail jams, park events, mountain comps, and indoor sessions near you.

Summer:

> Find skate jams, bike park events, gear swaps, and training sessions near you.

Fall:

> Track pre-season training, pass deadlines, gear deals, and opening day announcements.

Spring:

> Find slush cups, closing parties, skate events, gear sales, and MTB openings.

## Recommended Off-Season MVP Features

- Indoor training category
- Skate event category
- Gear swap category
- Mountain summer event category
- Gear deals
- Season pass deadline alerts
